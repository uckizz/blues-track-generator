Blues Jamtrack Generator v1.0

Select key, mode and tempo and the generator will find a jamtrack based on your criteria.

All the jamtracks are generated from a collection of youtube-links.

If no track is found, a random track in the same key will be generated.

If you feel wild and crazy, click the random button for a completely random track.
